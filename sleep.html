Sleep & Recovery

Build muscle faster with better rest and recovery practices.

Sleep Tips

  • 7-9 hours nightly for optimal repair and hormone balance.
  • Use a consistent schedule, reduce blue light before bed.
  • Maintain a cool, dark, quiet sleep environment.

Recovery Strategies

  • Light cardio, mobility, and foam rolling on rest days.
  • Deload every 4-6 weeks to avoid chronic fatigue.
  • Hydrate and eat protein after workouts consistently.
