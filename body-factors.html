Body Type Explainer

Adjust your training to your natural body tendencies.

Endomorph

Higher body fat, easy muscle gain; use calorie control and steady cardio.

Mesomorph

Muscular frame, responds fast to training; balanced volume and intensity works best.

Ectomorph

Lean, hardgainer; focus on calories, moderate volume, and recovery.
